Product description

Calcium Citrate (E333,CAS no.5785-44-4 HS code291815) is the calcium salt of citric acid, available as Fine white powder. Calcium Citrate along with citric acid, Sodium Citrate, Magnesium Citrate, Potassium Citrate is widely used as food acidity regulator. Calcium Citrate is also used as preservative in food.

Calcium citrate is also found in some dietary calcium supplements (e.g. Citracal). Calcium makes up 24.1% of calcium citrate (anhydrous) and 21.1% of calcium citrate (tetrahydrate) by mass. The tetrahydrate occurs in nature as the mineral Earlandite.
